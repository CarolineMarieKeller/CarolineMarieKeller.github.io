why we tabby.

tabby cats are made for companionship, we didn't know this when, years ago, Steve made his way into our lives. Over time, we've learned his insides must be made of sweet custard and all things wonderful - he's just so lovable! So, with our creation of Adopt-a-Tabby, we hope to give the joy of tabby cat companionship to others. With the large ferral community in Napa, there are plenty of tabby cats that need homes - will you open your home to one?
